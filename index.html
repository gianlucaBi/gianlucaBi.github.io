Short Bio

I am a postdoc in the Department of Electrical, Computer, and Energy Engineering at CU Boulder, working with Prof. Emiliano Dall'Anese. I completed my Ph.D. in the Department of Mechanical Engineering at UC Riverside in 2020, under the supervision of Prof. Fabio Pasqualetti. Before joining UC Riverside, I received a M.Sc. degree in Controls Engineering and a B.Sc. degree in Information Engineering from the University of Padova in 2014 and 2012, respectively.

Research Interests

My main research interests are in the modeling, analysis, and control of large-scale interconnected systems, with a focus on transportation networks.

Topics of current interest:

  • Modeling and optimization of traffic networks

  • Distributed control, optimization, and security in cyber-physical systems

  • Controllability and security of complex networks
