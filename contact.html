How to reach me

When travelling, please do not confuse Louvain-la-Neuve with Louvain, which is a different city.

Louvain-la-Neuve is a pedestrian city and you can get around simply on foot. Notice that a taxi will likely drop you off underground, this is because roads are under the city center.

Travelling by plane:

  • You can fly into Brussels Central (BRU) and take an Uber/Bolt or a taxi (approx 30 min) or a train (approx 80 min)

  • You can fly into Charleroi (CRL) and take a taxi (approx 40 min)

Travelling by train:

  • Set your destination to the station of 'Louvain-la-Neuve’ (about 40min from any central train station in Brussels)

Travelling by car:

  • The closest parking is here and free
